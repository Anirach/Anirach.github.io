Background

About Me

I am an Associate Professor specializing in computer networks, AI, and big data, with a Ph.D. from Liverpool John Moores University, UK.

Over the years, I have held key leadership roles at King Mongkut's University of Technology North Bangkok (KMUTNB), contributing to the advancement of industrial technology education. My research focuses on Agentic AI, wireless networking, information security, and cloud computing.

I actively contribute to the academic community as an IEEE/ACM reviewer and maintain numerous publications across international journals and conferences. I am dedicated to mentoring students and driving innovation in technology.
